David Rexford's Recipes

Buttercream

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz egg whites
  • 1 lb sugar
  • 2 1/2 lbs soft butter

Directions:

Place whites and sugar over a double boiler. Heat until whites are warm and the sugar is dissolved. Return to mixer with a whip attachment. Whip until bottom of bowl is cool. Fold in soft butter and mix well.

Chocolate Cake

(Preheat oven to 350; grease and paper pans)

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups AP flour
  • 3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 1/2 cups of buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 tsp vanilla

Directions:

Sift together all dry ingredients in one bowl. Mix together all wet ingredients in another bowl. Put dry mixture in mixing bowl with whip attachment. Add wet ingredients. Mix well. Divide mix into pans. Bake at 350 F for 30-35 minutes.
